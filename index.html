What is Mulank (Psychic Number) in Numerology?

Mulank, also known as the Psychic Number, is derived from your birth day (1–31 reduced to a single digit). It represents your inner nature, instincts, and how you emotionally respond to situations.

For example, if you are born on the 6th, your Mulank is 6. If born on the 24th, then 2 + 4 = 6. This number plays a key role in determining your personality traits and the type of energy that influences your daily life.

What Mulank Reveals:

  • Your personality and mindset
  • Emotional strengths and weaknesses
  • Natural talents and behavior patterns

What is Destiny Number (Life Path Number)?

The Destiny Number, also called the Life Path Number, is calculated using your full date of birth (DD/MM/YYYY). It reveals your life purpose, long-term goals, and the challenges you may face in your journey.

To calculate it, add all digits of your birth date and reduce them to a single digit. For example: 04/06/2026 → 0+4+0+6+2+0+2+6 = 20 → 2+0 = 2.

Destiny number is always reduced to a single digit (1–9) — ensuring precise crystal alignment and accurate numerology interpretation.

What Destiny Number Reveals:

  • Your life purpose and mission
  • Career direction and opportunities
  • Spiritual growth and karmic lessons
